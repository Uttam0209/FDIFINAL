Q1. How DO I use Show interest?

A1. As Per the search result, the portal will display appropriate product. The selected product will be added on show interest cart once the Add to show interest button is clicked..

Q2. What is make in India defence Portal?

A2. On the top of the website page there is an option provided Make In India Defence Portal .On Clicking the link the portal will be redirected to https://www.makeinindiadefence.gov.in/ portal.

Q3. What is DPSU login Bar?

A3. The Portal is used by DPSUs/OFB/SHQs for updating of the content available on the portal. The above stake holders may login in to the portal by clicking on the DPSU login .

Q4. What is Srijandefence portal?

A4. Srijandefence.gov.in portal provides opportunity for Indian Manufacturers to know the about the imported products .More Info can be obtained by clicking the About Us Page.

Q5. How do I use search option?

A5. At the top banner of the website there is a search option provided in which we can type minimum three character to find the related products as per your search query.

Q6.What are the available filters on srijandefence to use the filtering the available products ?

A6. On the left hand side of the portal, there are various filters provided, which help in finding the organisation products on the basis of:-

a.  Year of import:- 2018-19 2019-20 2020-21
b.  Annual Import Value (RS) :- 0.5-5 Million 5-10 Million 10-50 Million 50 Million Above
c.  Make in India target (starting) Year :- 2020-21 2021-22 2022-23 2023-24 2024-25
d.  Company :- GRSE OFB BEL BEML BDL GSL HAL HSL MDL MIDHANI SHQ (Air Force) SHQ (Army) SHQ (Navy)
e. NATO Supply Group Class:- In this all names of the NATO supply group class are mention in different categories, so that the user can identify easily in     which product users are interested.
f.  Make in India category:- iDEX/INNOVATION/R&D IGA IN HOUSE MAKE-II OTHER THAN MAKE –II The appropriate filters will give the products as result     of choosing the above filters.

Q7 How do I use More details option?

A7. On the right hand side of website there is a more details option provided, by clicking on that we can easily get the report on items year wise 2018-19, 2019-20,2020-21..
